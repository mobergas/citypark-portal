Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Who We Are

City Park Management LLC operates parking facilities and provides parking payment services through our platform at cityparkmanagement.app.

Information We Collect

When you use our parking payment system, we collect:

  • License plate number
  • Phone number (if provided for SMS alerts)
  • Email address (if provided for receipts)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details)
  • Vehicle type and parking session details

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your parking payment
  • Send SMS expiration alerts and receipts (only if you opt in)
  • Send email receipts (only if you provide your email)
  • Manage your monthly parking pass (if applicable)
  • Enforce parking rules and resolve disputes

SMS Communications

If you provide your phone number and check the SMS consent box, you agree to receive parking-related text messages from City Park Management LLC. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit or debit card information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data. You can view Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Twilio for SMS delivery
  • Property owners for parking enforcement purposes

Data Retention

We retain parking session records for up to 2 years for billing and dispute resolution purposes. Monthly pass records are retained for the duration of the pass and up to 1 year after cancellation.

Your Rights

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. We will respond within 30 days.
