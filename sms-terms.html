SMS Consent Terms

Last updated: July 2026

Program Description

City Park Holdings LLC sends SMS text messages to provide parking alerts, expiration reminders, and receipts for your parking session.

How to Opt In

By checking the SMS consent box on our payment screen and providing your mobile phone number, you agree to receive text messages from City Park Holdings LLC.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 3 messages per parking session (confirmation, expiration alert, receipt).

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any text message to unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Privacy

Your phone number will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
